# Kronos Tracker — Analytics Specification

## Current Analytics

- Daily completion score
- Weekly completion graph
- 14-day completion trend
- Study hours graph
- Subject balance chart
- Heatmap
- Current streak
- Longest streak
- 7-day average
- 30-day success rate
- Best day
- Weakest day
- Critical task rate
- Insight cards
- Mock score trend
- Mock accuracy trend

## Completion Analytics

Completion score is weighted by task priority.

## Time Analytics

Time is calculated from:

1. Completed task estimates
2. Focus timer/study session logs

The larger of the two is used for current MVP daily study time.

## Subject Balance

Subject balance is calculated across Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics using the last 7 days of subject minutes.

## Heatmap

Levels:

- 0: no data
- 1: weak day
- 2: medium day
- 3: good day
- 4: elite/perfect day

## Insights

Current insight types:

- Subject imbalance
- Focus score status
- Review pending/saved
- Recovery backlog warning
- Critical task status

## Future Analytics

- Best time of day
- Weakest time of day
- Task difficulty avoidance
- Monthly comparisons
- Burnout risk
- AI weekly review
- PDF progress report
